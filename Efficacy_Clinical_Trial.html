Important Safety Information
Contraindications
Some people have had new or worse mental health problems, such as changes in behavior or thinking, aggression, hostility, agitation, depressed mood, or suicidal thoughts or actions while taking or after stopping MILLIMAN. These symptoms happened more often in people who had a history of mental health problems. Stop taking MILLIMAN and call your healthcare provider right away if you, your family, or caregiver notice any of these symptoms. Before starting MILLIMAN, tell your healthcare provider if you ever had depression or other mental health problems.

Warnings and Precautions
New or worse heart or blood vessel problems can happen with MILLIMAN. Patients should tell their healthcare provider if they have heart or blood vessel problems or experience any symptoms during treatment. Patients should get emergency medical help right away if they have symptoms of a heart attack or stroke.
